The Arena

The arena is a 20x20, but the challenges within are unmeasurable....

Obstacles to look out for in the arena

  • Stone Walls

  • Brick Walls

  • Water

Treasures to hunt for

  • Life Packs

  • Random Treasures

  • Spoils of War

Setup

The Game takes place via communicating with a server using TCP connections. The server refreshes the game instance every second and sends updates on players, arena and the scores. The game is played by sending messages to the server about the next action.

Rules

  • A tanking hitting an obstacle will be penalized for each instance

  • A tank moving over water will be dead instantly

  • A bullet fired travels 3 times faster than the tanks

  • A bullet damages 10% of the initial health

  • A lifepack restores 20% of initial health

  • Tanks having 0% health die and drop their coins at the point of death

  • The game shall end when all the tanks are dead or all coins have disappeared or when the gametime is over

Scoring

Tanks can collect points

  • Breaking Bricks

  • Collects Random Treasures

  • Spoils of war

  • Killing an opponent (25% of the points of the victim)
